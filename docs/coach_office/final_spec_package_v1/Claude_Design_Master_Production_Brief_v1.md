# Claude Design Master Production Brief - Coach Office v1

## Goal
Create a premium, interactive Lake Tahoe coach office visual system for SquadVault.

## Style
- Warm Lake Tahoe lodge/executive office.
- Dark wood, leather, glass, pewter/silver, green accents.
- Cinematic, premium, nostalgic, adult, lightly whimsical.
- No real logos, no legible brand text, no hard-coded jokes in base art.
- Text and photos should be dynamically overlaid wherever possible.

## Required base scene
The hero office must include:
- Tahoe exterior view.
- Executive desk.
- Championship ring box on desk.
- Trophy case.
- Visible board/bulletin board/whiteboard surface.
- Framed photo areas.
- Desk drawer/receipts area.
- Shelves/books/lore cluster.
- Optional cigar/ashtray or whiskey glass.
- Optional cardboard cutout placement area.

## Required production variants
- Hero office default.
- Hero office with empty trophy case.
- Hero office with closed ring box.
- Hero office with blank board.
- Hero office populated example.
- Trophy case close-up empty.
- Trophy case close-up populated example.
- Ring box closed.
- Ring box open empty.
- Ring box open populated.
- Board close-up blank.
- Board close-up populated example.
- Desk drawer/receipts view.
- Photo gallery view.
- Cardboard cutout close-up/modal.

## Modular transparent assets
- Trophies, plaques, belts, rings, ring trays.
- Sticky notes, pins, tape, paper scraps, photo cards.
- Frame masks and glass glare overlays.
- Cigar/ashtray, whiskey glass, drawer contents, secret notes.
- Cardboard cutout templates and shadow/base stand.
- Tahoe day/evening/snow/summer view variants.

## Cutout rule
Cardboard cutouts are funny optional artifacts. They must be produced as reusable templates and transparent assets, not as baked-in PFL-only people.
